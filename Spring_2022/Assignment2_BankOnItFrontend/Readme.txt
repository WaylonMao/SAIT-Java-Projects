Project Title: Assignment 2 - Bank on It Frontend
Description: This program is a simple front-end that acts like an ATM (Automated Teller Machine) and connects using an existing backend. Users can log in by inputting their account numbers and pins to open their account window. The account window can show the account number, balance and transactions. On the bottom, there is an operation area for users to deposit or withdraw money. The transactions and balance will update in real time.
